About Us

Oconee County Fire Rescue (OCFR) is a mostly volunteer organization located in Ononee County, GA. In order to remain active, our fire fighters and rescue personnel must maintain various certifications, many of which expire after a few years. This website tracks and displays current OCFR members, their certifications, as well as their contact information!
